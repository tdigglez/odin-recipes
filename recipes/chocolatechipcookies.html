Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 1/4 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 tbsp of milk
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 or 2 cups of chocolate chips (personal preference)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375Â°
  2. Mix the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy (until dough forms)
  3. Add baking soda, salt, milk, and vanilla and mix slightly
  4. Add flour and mix
  5. Add egg and mix
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix
  7. Scoop 1/2 tbsp spoonful sized scoops onto baking sheet
  8. Bake in oven for 9 minutes
